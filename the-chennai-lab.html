The Chennai Lab

A home base for fieldwork on culture and money.

The Chennai Lab is the space where we explore the cultural significance behind financial behavior — does Chennai buy more gold during a recession? How is money actually handled inside the home? This is where those questions get asked, recorded, and studied.

PHOTO — The Chennai Lab workspace

How we work here

Fieldwork

Visiting homes, markets, and jewellers to hear how money decisions actually get made.

Interviews

Structured but conversational — recorded on voice and video, in Tamil and English.

Data review

Transcripts get coded into spreadsheets and reviewed for patterns across households.
