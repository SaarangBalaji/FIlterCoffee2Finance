Research

From conversations to data.

Our research starts as voice and video — interviews conducted in homes across South India. Each recording is transcribed, coded into structured spreadsheets, and analyzed for patterns before it becomes a published finding.

Our methodology

Step 1

Collect

Record voice and video interviews with households and individuals.

Step 2

Transcribe

Convert audio and video into written transcripts, in Tamil and English.

Step 3

Code

Structure quotes and figures into spreadsheets ready for analysis.

Step 4

Analyze

Look for patterns across households and publish what holds up.
