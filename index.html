A student-led research initiative

Where South Indian culture meets the economics of everyday money.

Filtercoffee2Finance is a field-research project studying how households across South India save, spend, and think about money — from gold-buying in a recession to how a family decides who holds the purse strings.

Explore the Research Visit The Chennai Lab

How we work

We record conversations, transcribe them into structured data, and look for patterns — treating oral history as seriously as a spreadsheet.

01

Listen

Voice and video interviews with families across Chennai and beyond, in Tamil and English.

02

Transcribe & Code

Recordings become structured spreadsheets — themes, amounts, decisions, and who made them.

03

Publish Findings

Patterns become short, plain-language insights and open data, published as they're ready.

The Chennai Lab

A home base for fieldwork on culture and money.

The Chennai Lab is where we run our interviews, review transcripts, and test ideas — does a recession change how much gold a family buys? How is money actually handled inside the home? This is the space where those questions get asked.
